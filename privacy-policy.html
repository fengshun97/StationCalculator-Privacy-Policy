Privacy Policy

App: Station Calculator
Package: com.station.petrolcalculator
Last updated: 14 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Station Calculator (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) handles information when you use the App. Station Calculator is a utility app that helps users estimate fuel subsidy calculations. It is an unofficial tool and is not affiliated with any government agency.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or payment details.

The App may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Pump price preference — saved so you do not need to re-enter it
  • Language preference — English or Bahasa Melayu

This information stays on your device and is not uploaded to our own servers. We do not operate a backend server for this App.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to show advertisements. Google and its partners may collect and process certain information to deliver and measure ads, which may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device and app information
  • Ad interaction and performance data

This data may be used for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, and related purposes by Google/AdMob according to Google’s policies.

Learn more here: Google Privacy Policy and How AdMob uses data .

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide core App features (calculation and saved preferences)
  • To remember your language and pump price settings
  • To display ads through Google AdMob

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Advertising-related data may be processed by Google AdMob and its partners as part of ad delivery and measurement.

5. Data Retention

Local preferences remain on your device until you clear the App’s data or uninstall the App. Advertising data handled by Google is retained according to Google’s own retention practices.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general adult users and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information processed by the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

8. Your Choices

  • You can clear App data or uninstall the App to remove locally stored preferences.
  • On Android, you can reset or limit your Advertising ID in system settings (Settings → Google → Ads, or equivalent on your device).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when changes are made. Continued use of the App after updates means you accept the revised policy.

11. Official Government Sources

Subsidy rates and programme names used in the App are based on publicly available information from Malaysian government programmes. Station Calculator is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by any government agency.

Official sources for subsidy information (public Ministry of Finance pages, readable without login):

  • MoF press release — BUDI95 / SKPS petrol rates (includes subsidised RON95 RM1.99 and SKPS RM2.05)
  • MoF press release — targeted diesel subsidy / SKDS rates (includes SKDS RM2.15 and land public transport RM1.88)
  • MoF announcement PDF — BUDI MADANI Diesel (includes BUDI Diesel RM2.10)
Disclaimer: Station Calculator provides estimates only. Fuel subsidy rates and eligibility rules may change. The App is not an official government service. Always verify final amounts at the pump or with the official sources above.
